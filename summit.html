PRAYATNA SUMMIT 2025 - India's First Student Welfare Summit

About the Summit

The Student Welfare Board of IIT Guwahati proudly presents Prayatna Summit 2025, India's first ever Student Welfare Summit, scheduled from 7th to 9th March 2025. This landmark event will bring together over 250 student representatives from 50+ universities and colleges across Northeast India, fostering collaboration, dialogue, and action to enhance student well-being.

Set against the beautiful backdrop of IIT Guwahati's scenic campus, the summit will feature panel discussions, round-table conferences, interactive stalls, career-focused sessions, and wellness activities, creating an engaging and transformative experience for all participants.

What to Expect?

Prayatna Summit 2025 is designed to address the key challenges and opportunities that shape student life, academics, research, and career development. Through expert-led discussions and student-driven initiatives, the summit will pave the way for impactful policy changes and a more enriching educational ecosystem.
