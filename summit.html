PAN - INDIA COLLEGE WELFARE SUMMIT

We are proud to announce the Pan North-East India Welfare Expo, a grand and comprehensive event dedicated to fostering discussions on student welfare, security, and well-being across educational institutions. This landmark gathering will bring together representatives from over 50 prestigious colleges and universities spanning the entire North-East and East India region.
The primary objective of this expo is to establish a collaborative platform where academicians, student leaders, welfare officers, and policymakers can engage in insightful discussions about the pressing challenges faced by students today. These include but are not limited to:

• Student Security & Well-Being: Addressing concerns related to campus safety, mental health support, and anti-ragging measures.
• Attendance & Academic Performance: Exploring innovative strategies to improve student engagement, attendance policies, and academic performance tracking.
• Financial Assistance & Scholarships: Providing guidance on scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities to support students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.
• Hostel & Accommodation Facilities: Evaluating the quality of hostel infrastructure, grievance redressal mechanisms, and ways to improve the residential experience for students.
• Career Guidance & Skill Development: Introducing career counseling initiatives, internship opportunities, and skill-enhancement programs to empower students for future success.
• Digital Learning & Technological Integration: Discussing the role of technology in education, online learning platforms, and digital transformation in universities.
• Mental Health & Counseling Support: Highlighting the importance of mental health awareness, stress management programs, and the availability of counseling services for students.

This event will also serve as an excellent opportunity for networking, allowing student representatives, faculty members, and administrative heads to exchange ideas, share best practices, and devise innovative solutions to enhance student life across various institutions.

With interactive panel discussions, keynote sessions by eminent educationists, live workshops, and policy recommendation forums, the Pan North-East India Welfare Expo will pave the way for a more inclusive, student-friendly, and progressive education ecosystem.

We invite all stakeholders in the education sector—students, faculty, administrators, policymakers, and NGOs—to be a part of this groundbreaking initiative that aims to redefine student welfare and create a brighter future for the youth of North-East and East India.
